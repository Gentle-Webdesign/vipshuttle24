## Design Philosophy: Modern Luxury

## 🎨 Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Obsidian Black** (#0A0A0A) - Primary background, creates depth and sophistication
- **Deep Charcoal** (#1C1C1C) - Secondary backgrounds, adds subtle contrast
- **Champagne Gold** (#D4AF37) - Premium accent, represents luxury and exclusivity
- **Metallic Silver** (#C0C0C0) - Subtle highlights, adds elegance
- **Pure White** - Text and key elements, ensures readability

### Typography
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (Elegant serif)
  - Conveys sophistication and timelessness
  - Used for headlines and key messaging
  
- **Body Text**: Inter (Modern sans-serif)
  - Highly readable at all sizes
  - Professional and clean appearance
